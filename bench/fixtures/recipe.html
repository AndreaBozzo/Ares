Weeknight Lemon Garlic Orzo

By Priya Raman

A bright, one-pan orzo with garlic, lemon, and parmesan — on the table in half an hour.

  • Cuisine: Mediterranean
  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Cook time: 20 mins
  • Total time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 4 servings
  • Rating: 4.8

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups orzo
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast the orzo in olive oil over medium heat until golden, about 2 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, 30 seconds.
  3. Pour in 3 cups water, bring to a simmer, and cook until the orzo is tender, 12–15 minutes.
  4. Stir in the lemon zest, lemon juice, parmesan, and spinach until wilted.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve warm.
