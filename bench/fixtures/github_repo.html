Tempest

Tempest schedules and runs background jobs across a cluster of workers. It uses a Postgres-backed queue for durability, a gossip protocol for worker discovery, and a circuit breaker to shed load from failing downstreams. Jobs are delivered at least once; handlers are expected to be idempotent.

Features

  • At-least-once delivery with configurable retry and backoff
  • Cron and one-shot scheduling
  • Horizontal scaling with no single coordinator
  • Graceful shutdown and in-flight job draining
